# **App Name**: NudgeCraft

## Core Features:

- Landing Screen: Minimalist landing with a concise thesis and a 'Start' CTA.
- Concept Primer: Informative screens detailing motivation, resistance, autonomy, and understanding.
- Interactive Scenario: Realistic scenario where users select supportive responses to disengagement, with feedback on each choice.
- Reflection Screen: Synthesis of effective strategies and reusable principles, plus a 'Replay' option.

## Style Guidelines:

- Background color: Dark grey (#2B2B2B) for a calm and trustworthy feel.
- Primary color: Muted blue (#64B5F6) suggesting intelligence and emotional maturity.
- Accent color: Soft yellow (#FFEB3B) used sparingly for highlighting key feedback points.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif for a modern and objective look.
- Apple-like restraint with plenty of white space to feel calm and intentional.
- Soft, subtle transitions to enhance the modern feel without being distracting.
- Minimalist iconography with thin strokes to maintain a clean and modern aesthetic.
